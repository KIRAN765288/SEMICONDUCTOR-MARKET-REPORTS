Surround Sound Preamps Market Overview
Surround Sound Preamps are the brains of all mid to high end home theater systems. They are similar to AV receivers in that they provide source switching and decoding, audio/video processing and volume control for various surround sound formats. But unlike an AV receiver, a Surround Sound Preamp has no built-in amplification and is dedicated to serving as the "brain" of the system. This means they generally have more processing power available. Includes additional channels, flexible adjustment and calibration options. A Surround Sound Preamp is a high-end audio component that processes and decodes audio signals in a home theater or commercial audio setup. Unlike AV receivers, preamps do not have built-in amplifiers and provide superior sound processing capabilities.
