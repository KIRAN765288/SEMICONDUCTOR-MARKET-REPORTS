Multilayer SMD Varistor Market Overview
Multilayer SMD Varistor is a passive electronic component designed to protect electronic circuits from overvoltage. It consists of multiple stacked semiconductor ceramic layers that absorb and clamp high voltage transients to prevent circuit damage. What is a Multilayer SMD Varistor?
A Multilayer SMD Varistor is a surface-mount passive electronic component designed to protect circuits from transient voltage spikes. It consists of multiple stacked ceramic semiconductor layers that absorb excess voltage and prevent circuit damage.

How does the Multilayer SMD Varistor enhance electronic device safety?
