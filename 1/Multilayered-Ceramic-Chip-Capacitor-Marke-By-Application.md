Multilayered Ceramic Chip Capacitor Market Overview
Multilayer ceramic chip capacitors, or MLCCs, are important building blocks in today's modern electronics and make up approximately 30% of the total components in a typical hybrid circuit module. Multilayer capacitors consist of a monolithic ceramic block with comb-like sintered electrodes. 2. What are Multilayered Ceramic Chip Capacitors (MLCCs)?

MLCCs are compact, highly reliable capacitors made of multiple ceramic layers with embedded electrodes. They play a crucial role in modern electronics by offering high capacitance, stability, and low losses.
