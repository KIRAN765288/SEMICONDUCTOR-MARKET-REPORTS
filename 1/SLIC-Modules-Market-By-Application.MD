SLIC Modules Market Overview
Subscriber Line Interface Circuit (SLIC) modules are used for analogue telephone line interface applications,connecting to a standard analogue telephone handset. They enable signals to be transmitted and received, power to be fed to the line and monitoring of the line condition. The modules are used in applications such as Alarm Systems, Elevator telephones, ISDN Terminal Adaptors, Internet Telephony (VoIP), Computer Telephony Integration (CTI), Wireless Local Loop (WLL), Fixed Cellular Terminals (FCT) and Small Office Home Office (SOHO).This report provides a deep insight into the global SLIC Modules market covering all its essential aspects.
