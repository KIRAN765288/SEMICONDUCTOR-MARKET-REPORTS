Market Segmentation

Understanding the market segmentation helps to identify the specific drivers behind the growth of RTD temperature sensors:
By Type:

Wire-Wound RTD
Thin Film RTD
Wire-wound RTDs are known for their accuracy and stability, making them ideal for critical applications, while thin-film RTDs offer compactness and cost-efficiency.
By Application:

Automotive Industry: Used in engine management systems to ensure optimal performance.
HVAC: Critical for monitoring and controlling heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems.
Medical: Essential for temperature monitoring in diagnostic and therapeutic devices.
Food Industry: Ensures compliance with safety standards during food production and storage.
Others: Includes aerospace, energy, and research sectors.
