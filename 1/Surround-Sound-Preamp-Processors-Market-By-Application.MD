Surround Sound Preamp Processors Market Overview
Surround Sound Preamp Processors are the brains of all mid to high end home theater systems. They are similar to AV receivers in that they provide source switching and decoding, audio/video processing and volume control for various surround sound formats. But unlike an AV receiver, a Surround Sound Preamp Processors has no built-in amplification and is dedicated to serving as the "brain" of the system. This means they generally have more processing power available. Includes additional channels, flexible adjustment and calibration options. What is a Surround Sound Preamp Processor?
A Surround Sound Preamp Processor is a high-end audio component that acts as the control center for home theater systems. Unlike AV receivers, they do not have built-in amplification, allowing them to focus entirely on signal processing and offering superior sound quality and customization options.
