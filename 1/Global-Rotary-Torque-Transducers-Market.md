Market Segmentation
By Type:

Dynamic Type: Suited for measuring torque in rotating systems, this segment caters to industries requiring real-time torque monitoring.
Static Type: Ideal for stationary applications, static torque transducers are widely used in test and measurement scenarios.
By Application:

Automotive: Essential for drivetrain and engine testing, ensuring vehicle safety and efficiency.
Aerospace: Used in aircraft component testing and propulsion systems for enhanced reliability.
Marine: Facilitates torque measurement in propulsion and power systems.
Industrial Machinery: Drives operational efficiency in manufacturing and processing equipment.
Others: Includes applications in renewable energy and research sectors.
