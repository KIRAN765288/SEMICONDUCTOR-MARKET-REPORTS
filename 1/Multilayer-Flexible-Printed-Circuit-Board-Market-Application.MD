1. What is a Multilayer Flexible Printed Circuit Board (FPCB)?
Answer: A Multilayer Flexible Printed Circuit Board is a type of circuit board that consists of three or more conductive layers (usually copper foil) pressed together. These layers are bonded by a material called prepreg, creating a complex and flexible electronic system used in various devices, from consumer electronics to medical equipment.
