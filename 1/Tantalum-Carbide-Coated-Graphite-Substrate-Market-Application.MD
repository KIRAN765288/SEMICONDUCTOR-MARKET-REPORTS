1. What is Tantalum Carbide (TaC) Coated Graphite Substrate?

Tantalum Carbide (TaC) Coated Graphite Substrate is a high-performance material prepared by coating high-purity graphite with tantalum carbide using processes like CVD, PVD, or sintering. It offers excellent thermal stability, high purity, and superior conductivity, making it ideal for high-temperature applications.
