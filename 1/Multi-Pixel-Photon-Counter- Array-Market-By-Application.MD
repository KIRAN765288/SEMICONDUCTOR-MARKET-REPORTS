1. What is a Multi-Pixel Photon Counter (MPPC) Array?

MPPC, also known as a Silicon Photomultiplier (SiPM), is a solid-state photomultiplier that consists of a high-density matrix of Geiger-mode avalanche photodiodes (SPADs). It enables single-photon detection with high internal gain.

8. What challenges does the MPPC Array market face?

9. What is the role of MPPC technology in LiDAR applications?

MPPC technology enables high-precision 3D ranging and imaging, making it essential for automotive LiDAR, robotics, and industrial automation. It offers superior sensitivity and compact design compared to traditional photomultiplier tubes.
